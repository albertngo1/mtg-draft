## Format in one line

**Ten clearly-defined two-color synergy archetypes** — one per guild, each anchored by a signpost uncommon. Per Draftsim this is the first set since Avatar: The Last Airbender to ship a full 10 supported archetypes, and "so far it looks like these strategies are well supported and balanced." The format is built around **superhero flavor mechanics** (Teamwork, Power-up, connive, hero/villain typal payoffs) rather than a single overriding theme.

Format notes the guide leans on:
- **Pick the synergy, then build into it** — every guild has a distinct, narrow payoff (attack-alone, draw-two, two-creatures-in-graveyard, etc.); the article frames the format as synergy-focused deckbuilding.
- **Some signposts are generically strong** — e.g. "Kang, Temporal Tyrant is another card that's just generally good on its own," functioning as both enabler and payoff.
- The article is an **archetype map**, not a card-quality tier list: it does **not** name a removal suite, rank commons, or call out non-signpost bombs. Those rankings are deliberately omitted here rather than invented.

## Mechanics

The article gives only partial detail on how the keywords function (spoiler-season speculation):

- **Teamwork** (UW) — rewards tapping your own creatures / cooperative effects; Captain America, Living Legend "gives all of your creatures pseudo-vigilance."
- **Connive** (UB, also GU) — the engine behind the draw-two and counters plans; UB "will care a lot about drawing two cards… mostly through cards that connive."
- **Power-up** (RG, GU) — grows creatures; RG either plays creatures that are "just huge by default, or creatures you grow with their Power-up abilities."
- **Villain typal** (BR) — payoffs scale with "as many good villains in your deck as possible."
- **Hero typal** (GW) — reward for playing "as many hero creatures as possible."
- **Two-or-more-creatures-in-graveyard** (BG) — "an interesting twist on graveyard synergy, caring specifically about having two or more creature cards in your graveyard."
- **Attack-alone** (WB) — constraint payoff: triggers "when you attack with only one creature."
- **Heroic-like / noncreature-cast** (RW) — "cares about casting noncreature spells, which includes equipment and payoffs in the form of 'heroic'-like creatures."
- **Artifacts** (UR) — high artifact density.

## Archetypes

### Azorius Teamwork (UW)
- **Signpost:** Captain America, Living Legend.
- **Plan:** combine spells with Teamwork effects and creatures you can tap; Cap "gives all of your creatures pseudo-vigilance" and leads the team.
- **Key cards:** Agent Maria Hill, Murdock's Crusade.

### Dimir Draw-Two (UB)
- **Signpost:** Kang, Temporal Tyrant.
- **Plan:** care about drawing two cards in a turn, mostly via connive; Kang is "both an enabler and a payoff" and "generally good on its own."
- **Key cards:** Construct a Cosmic Cube, Leader, Super-Genius.

### Rakdos Villains (BR)
- **Signpost:** Madame Hydra.
- **Plan:** "putting as many good villains in your deck as possible" to maximize villain synergies.
- **Key cards:** The Masters of Evil, Doom Reigns Supreme.

### Gruul Power-up Stompy (RG)
- **Plan:** "go big or go home" — smash with creatures that are huge by default or grown via Power-up abilities.
- **Signpost:** Hulk, Gamma Goliath.
- **Key cards:** HULK SMASH!, Doc Samson, Super Psychiatrist.

### Selesnya Heroes (GW)
- **Signpost:** Black Panther, Vanguard.
- **Plan:** "play as many hero creatures as possible" to trigger hero-typal payoffs.
- **Key cards:** Agent Phil Coulson, Avengers Assemble!.

### Orzhov Attack Alone (WB)
- **Signpost:** Black Widow, Double Agent.
- **Plan:** a constraint deck that rewards you "when you attack with only one creature."
- **Key cards:** Agent 13, Sharon Carter; Luke Cage, Power Man.

### Golgari Graveyard (BG)
- **Signpost:** Killmonger, Scourge of Wakanda.
- **Plan:** "an interesting twist on graveyard synergy" — wants two or more creature cards in the graveyard for triggers.
- **Key cards:** HYDRA Troopers, Punishing Punch.

### Simic Counters (GU)
- **Signpost:** Ant-Man, Colony Commander.
- **Plan:** build board presence through +1/+1 counter synergies fed by connive and Power-up.
- **Key cards:** Hellcat, Undying Vigilante; Go Nuts!.

### Izzet Artifacts (UR)
- **Signpost:** Iron Man, Master of Machines.
- **Plan:** Izzet takes a break from spellslinging to go high artifact density; Iron Man is "both an enabler and a payoff."
- **Key cards:** Shuri, Wakandan Inventor; Armor Wars.

### Boros Noncreature Spells (RW)
- **Signpost:** Thor Odinson.
- **Plan:** cast noncreature spells (including equipment) to trigger "heroic"-like creatures.
- **Key cards:** Iron Fist, Living Weapon; Colleen Wing, Street Samurai.
