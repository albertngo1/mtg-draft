# DSK (Duskmourn: House of Horror) — Numot draft notes
> Source: NumotTheNummy (Kenji Egashira) regular Arena draft VODs — sampled 4 end-of-format VODs (tier-2 set, thinner coverage). Built 2026-06-10 from YouTube auto-caption transcripts; card/mechanic names corrected where confident, garbled readings marked (?).

## Format speed / meta read
- Kenji drafted this as a flashback format (returning to it after Aetherdrift). Describes the format as deep and varied, with several viable archetypes.
- **White-blue** is called "maybe the best deck in the format" — the Gremlin Tamer / enchantment deck, not rooms. Kenji confirmed this explicitly.
- **Red-blue Rooms** is described as viable only if you get the correct pieces; everyone fights for them and it often doesn't come together. Promissory Stairs (win-con room that wins if you have 8+ different unlocked room names) is a meme build but Kenji won with it when he hit 5 Scorching Dragonfires.
- **Red-white** is a real aggro archetype. "Arabella" (2-mana 2/2 with relevant abilities?) was the missing piece Kenji noted in one RW draft.
- **Black-white** is the reanimate/enchantment value deck. Shroud Stomper is the premier reanimate target; Right of the Moth and Faithful (sorcery speed reanimate 1/1) are the engines.
- **Red-green** is a legitimate pressuring deck; described as having punishing fast curves.
- **Black-red sacrifice** is one of Kenji's favorites — "one of my favorite archetypes in the format." Vengeful Possession and Disturbing Mirth (?) as key cards.
- Roller Crusher Ride (red mythic) is described as "one of the best rares/mythics you can open in DSK." 3 mana, kills many creatures, and with delirium doubles non-combat damage. "Splashable and certainly the pick."
- Delirium (4+ card types in graveyard) is a key mechanic. Enchantment cyclers and rooms count toward this.
- Defiant Survivor (?) — white 3-mana 3/2 that triggers when any enchantment enters — was described as "one of the biggest bombs in the format" and "check on the format." Kenji dreaded opposing this card; says if they play Survivor and you miss your land drop, you're just losing.

## Archetypes
- **White-blue enchantments / Gremlin Tamer(?):** Best deck per Kenji. Glimmer Light (2-mana 1/1 that creates a 1/1 Gremlin token with enchantment triggers), Gremlin Tamer(?), Grand Entryway (room that makes tokens), Erratic Apparition(?), Unsettling Twins (4-mana manifest two cards). High synergy between enchantments entering and getting a creature/pump.
- **Black-white midrange / reanimate:** Shroud Stomper (6-mana 4/5 flyer, returns when it dies if you have 4+ card types in graveyard?), Right of the Moth (sorcery that mills 3 then reanimates a creature from your graveyard — flashback in opposite colors), Faithful (1/1, when it dies reanimates a creature from graveyard), Emerge from the Cocoon (another reanimate spell). Pair with Unsettling Twins to manifest dread (put face-down cards, often hitting big creatures to discard and reanimate). Fanatic of Harrowing(?), Doll (split-skin doll — 2/1 that loots when it enters, can be exiled and replayed), Cynical Loner(?)(2-mana 3/1 that tutors a card to graveyard on tapped trigger), Alton-Ax(?) (big green creature that can self-discard from hand if you have a Forest). Key enabler for self-mill.
- **Red-white aggro:** Percussionist (1-mana 1/1), Doll (2-mana looting two-drop), Horde Caller (5-mana 4/4 haste, makes Gremlins whenever it attacks), Glimmer Light, Hand That Feeds(?)(enchantment creature that gives a bonus), Fear of Being Hunted(?)(enchantment creature with evasion), Jump Scare (instant +2/+2 flying — good trick). Surgical Suite(?) returns a 3-or-less creature from graveyard. Patched Plaything(?) — enters with minus counters if cast from hand, but is clean when exiled and replayed.
- **Red-black sacrifice:** Vengeful Possession (discard payoff), Disturbing Mirth(?) (bomb red card — likely mass damage or act-of-treason type effect), Egotist of Harrowing(?)(sacrifice outlet).
- **Red-blue Rooms:** Smoky Lounge + Misty Salon (red rooms that make mana or creatures), Central Elevator + Central Staircase (room win-con — 2 rooms per card), Underwater Tunnel (blue room, essential for the win condition), Scorching Dragonfire (removal + cycling), Pyroclasm, Painter's Studio (land + card draw). Promising Stairs wins if you have 8+ different names among unlocked doors — requires 4 different room names unlocked, each room card has 2 rooms. At best a meme, but functional if you hit 4-5 Dragonfires as backup.

## Card interactions & combos
- **Right of the Moth + Shroud Stomper:** Mill 3 to graveyard, reanimate Shroud Stomper. Flashback let you do it again from graveyard, though in opposite colors. Put finality counter on reanimated creature so it doesn't return again — plan accordingly.
- **Unsettling Twins + reanimate effects:** Twins manifest two cards face-down; creatures manifested face-down and then killed/flipped can be reanimated. Also: put a big creature face-down, let it die, reanimate with Right of the Moth.
- **Doll + Surgical Suite(?):** Exile the doll with Surgical Suite to cast a 3-or-less creature from the graveyard, then replay the Doll as a 2/1 (no minus counters since it wasn't cast from hand).
- **Savior of the Small(?)(2-mana 2/2) + stick twister(?) / creature chain:** Savior returns a creature to hand when it blocks/dies. Kenji used this to loop value creatures.
- **Shattered Yard (room) + any damage source:** Room deals 1 damage on upkeep or when something dies. With Roller Crusher Ride, any non-combat damage doubles with delirium — the yard pings becoming lethal.
- **Untimely Malfunction:** Changes target of a spell or ability to a single target. Redirect opponent's removal to kill their own creature. "When it's good, it's insane." Opponent redirected Kenji's own pump, swinging a game completely.
- **Horde Caller + Midnight Mayhem(?) + Gremlins:** Horde Caller makes a Gremlin each time it attacks. Mayhem(?) gets better with more Gremlins in play.
- **Norin + Patched Plaything(?):** Norin(?) exiles permanents temporarily. If you exile Patched Plaything with Norin and return it, the play thing re-enters without the minus counters (it only gets those when cast from hand) — effectively a 4/3.

## Card tips
- **Roller Crusher Ride:** Take it over almost everything; "one of the best cards in the format." Splashable. Even without delirium it's 3 mana deal 3 and kill a creature; with delirium all non-combat damage doubles.
- **Scorching Dragonfire:** "One of the better red commons." Take multiples; Kenji had 5 in one draft and called it "anything's possible." Hits creatures AND enchantments.
- **Shroud Stomper:** Top-end black-white. 6-mana 4/5 flyer that returns from graveyard under certain conditions. Dream reanimate target.
- **Unsettling Twins:** High pick in any deck that wants graveyard action. Manifest two is powerful setup.
- **Doll (split-skin doll?):** Good 2-drop loot effect. Pairs with Surgical Suite and Norin for flickering tricks.
- **Horde Caller:** 5-mana 4/4 haste that generates Gremlins on each attack — it doesn't have to attack itself. Very strong. "Horde Caller was really good."
- **Glimmer Light:** 2-mana 1/1 that makes a 1/1 Gremlin on attack (doesn't have to attack itself). Core of the white-blue enchantment deck.
- **Meat Locker (room):** Blue common room, taken highly. Taps a creature when the room is opened. Kenji noted missing it specifically in the rooms deck draft.
- **Grand Entryway (room):** Makes multiple enchantments, triggers enchantment-matters effects.
- **Murder:** Always good. Take it over almost all on-color two-drops.
- **Unsettling Twins + manifest:** Value in any deck that discards. Good with Fanatic of Harrowing(?) to pitch large creatures.
- **Pyroclasm:** Good in rooms deck to clear small creatures (0/2 and 1/1s). Also red-blue rooms use it.
- **Defiant Survivor:** "One of the most annoying cards to face." 3/2 that makes tokens/pumps on each enchantment entering. Kenji dreaded seeing it and lost multiple games to it.
- **Final Vengeance:** Black 3-mana exile removal. Kenji played it at high pick rate, mentions it as reliable removal. Can sacrifice an enchantment to cast it (enabling delirium).
- **Nowhere to Run:** Solid black removal, "the correct pick" often when ahead on enchantments.

## Pitfalls
- **Red-blue rooms without enough different room names:** Need 4 different room names (each card has 2) to enable Promising Stairs. Common rooms (Meat Locker, Underwater Tunnel, Bottomless Pool) are heavily contested. If you miss them, the win-con doesn't work. Backup plan: 5 Dragonfires.
- **Reanimate deck without discard outlets:** Kenji explicitly said "I needed some more fanatic of harrowing... right now it's just goat, doll, overdrawing as my three discard effects." Without ways to put big creatures in the graveyard, the reanimate plan stalls.
- **Right of the Moth flashback cost:** Flashback is 2W1B (reversed from casting cost). Planning to use the flashback requires both white and black open. Don't assume you can flash it back without the right mana.
- **Finality counters on reanimated creatures:** Right of the Moth puts finality counters on returned creatures. You cannot reanimate the same creature multiple times with these effects.
- **Roller Crusher Ride but no delirium:** Still good at 3 mana deal-3 but substantially worse. Prioritize getting 4 card types in the graveyard (creature, instant, enchantment/sorcery, land usually).
- **Overextending into rooms too early:** Rooms can be sacrificed by opponent (Glassworks-type effects). Don't lock all your mana into rooms when opponent might have interaction.
- **Boros missing Arabella:** Red-white aggro needs its key 2-drop. Without a critical-mass of 2-drops, the curve is gappy and you can't apply enough early pressure.
